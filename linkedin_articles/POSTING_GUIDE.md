## üí° Pro Tips

### Maximize Engagement

1. **Post at optimal times:** 8-9 AM EST on weekdays
2. **Use native LinkedIn articles:** Better reach than external links
3. **Engage immediately:** First 2 hours are critical
4. **Ask questions:** End posts with questions to encourage comments
5. **Tag strategically:** 2-4 relevant people/orgs per post
6. **Cross-promote:** Share each article multiple times over weeks

### Build Momentum

1. **Tease next article:** Create anticipation
2. **Reference previous articles:** Build narrative arc
3. **Highlight engagement:** Share interesting comments/discussions
4. **Create urgency:** Limited opportunities, timely topics
5. **Show progress:** GitHub stars, collaborations, milestones

### Avoid Common Mistakes

1. **Don't over-tag:** Looks spammy
2. **Don't ignore comments:** Kills engagement
3. **Don't post and ghost:** Stay active for 2-4 hours
4. **Don't use too many hashtags:** 5-7 max
5. **Don't be salesy:** Focus on value, not promotion
